# Decision: Seismic Plywood Sheathing Requires Permit + Plan

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Source:** SF DBI permit counter (in person, 2026-03-25)

## Finding

DBI confirmed that installing structural plywood sheathing on the garage wall **requires a building permit** and **requires a plan** (not just an OTC application without plans).

This means it's a **Form 3** (other agencies review required) process, not Form 8 (OTC without plans).

## What This Means

- The Form 3/8 application we prepared can still be used, but needs to be submitted as Form 3 (check Form 3 instead of Form 8)
- A plan/drawing must be submitted showing the sheathing layout, nailing schedule, plywood grade, and which wall(s) are affected
- This will go through plan review, which takes longer than OTC
- May require an engineer's stamp depending on DBI's requirements
